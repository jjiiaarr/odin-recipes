Carbonara

    Ingredients :

  • 250 grams Spaghetti
  • 3/4 cup crispy bacon bits
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Cold Water
  • 1 packet NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream, 125ml, chilled
  • 4 tablespoons melting cheese, grated
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

    Procedure :

  1. Boil pasta according to package direction. (10 minutes)
  2. Cook bacon in a pan to render fat. Strain bacon bits and set aside. (5 minutes)
  3. Sauté garlic in the same pan. Pour water and NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream. Stir in cheese and simmer. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap® and pepper. (10 minutes)
  4. Strain spaghetti and toss in the sauce. Transfer on a serving plate and top with bacon bits. Serve immediately. (5 minutes)
